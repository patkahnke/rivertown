About Rivertown Communications

Rivertown Communications, Inc. develops custom e-Learning training programs and distance learning systems for businesses and government agencies. We are located in Stillwater, Minnesota (near St. Paul / Minneapolis), and have been serving clients locally and worldwide since 1991. Our customers range in size from small, single-office organizations to global corporations to State and Federal government agencies. Whatever your training and education needs are - we can help.

What we do

Rivertown Communications excels at creating effective e-learning and video based training courses using the content and knowledge your organization already has or we can develop new material using our experts. We develop training that helps your employees, affiliates and learners become more effective. We begin by meeting with your staff to listen to your needs and to identify the content for your training. Our goal is to make sure the time and resources you dedicate to your training program is 100% effective.

Our instructional designers will write custom lessons targeted to your precise needs and our graphics designers and video professionals will create the visual elements to best illustrate the training. Our programmers will combine all elements into an e-Learning program or video production that is available to your audience 24/7. If desired, testing and learner tracking can be integrated into the training modules to measure progress and certify mastery. Rivertown Communications will work with your staff every step of the way and your team can be involved as little or as much as desired.

Our Typical Customer

We work with small organizations and large government agencies and corporations. Our typical customer is one who has identified a training need within their organization but does not have available staff to develop, test and implement the training themselves.

Rivertown Communications will work with your staff as part of your training .development team and can fulfill all your development needs, from producing an e-Learning module or video production from your script to handling the complete production including research, identifying content and instructional design.
